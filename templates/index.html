Password Tips
  • Include numbers, symbols, and both uppercase and lowercase letters.
  • Never use personal information such as your name, birthday, user name, or email address. This type of information is often publicly available.
  • Use a longer password. Your password should be at least six characters long, although for extra security it should be even longer.
  • Don't use the same password for each account. If your password for one account is discovered, all of your other accounts will be vulnerable.
  • Avoid using words that can be found in the dictionary. For example, walking123 would be a weak password.
